High-Quality Earnings Momentum

Quantitative Portfolio Management · Equity Strategy

This group project studies a long-short equity strategy built around earnings surprise, profitability, and asset growth. The idea is to test whether the market underreacts more strongly when firms combine high-quality earnings with conservative growth, and whether that interaction can generate persistent alpha.

CBDC vs. Private Stablecoin

Digital Assets · Monetary Systems · Policy Analysis

This presentation compares central bank digital currencies and private stablecoins through a U.S.-focused lens. It examines their differences as forms of public and private money, compares cross-country developments in the U.S., Europe, and China, and discusses why the U.S. may remain more supportive of stablecoins than CBDCs in the near term.
